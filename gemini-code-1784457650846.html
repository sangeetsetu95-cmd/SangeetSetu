Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Welcome to Sangeet Setu. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the app, create a profile, or interact with other users. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and contact details.
  • Profile pictures and media uploads.
  • Chat messages and booking details generated within the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the features and functionality of Sangeet Setu, including:

  • Facilitating user connections, communication, and bookings.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Ensuring legal compliance and protecting against fraudulent activity.

3. Firebase Services

We utilize Google Firebase services (such as Firebase Authentication, Firestore, or Realtime Database) to securely handle user authentication, store application data, and ensure smooth functionality. Data processed via Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cloudinary Image Storage

To provide high-quality media management, profile images and other media assets uploaded by users are stored securely using Cloudinary. Cloudinary processes this media solely to host and deliver content efficiently within the app.

5. Booking & Chat Data

Any details related to bookings and chat conversations between users are processed to facilitate the core services of Sangeet Setu. While we employ security protocols to protect this transmission, users are advised not to share sensitive financial or highly personal data over chat.

6. Notifications

We may send you push notifications or alerts regarding updates, booking status changes, or relevant interactions within the app. You can manage or turn off these notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Data Security

We implement a variety of standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, please note that no electronic transmission or storage technology can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Children's Privacy

Sangeet Setu does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
